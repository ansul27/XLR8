About XLR8

Project Description:

XLR8's mission is to help small to mid-sized manafacturing companies stay competitive by implementing voice-controlled non-intrustive technology to assist them in accurately tracking the time it takes to complete work orders for their clients.

Why Users Should Care:

Users should care about this because this implementation of voice-controlled time tracking is a non-intrusive method of accurately determining how long it takes for manual work orders to be completed inside of manfacturing plants. While the product we're developing mainly caters to the manafacturing plants that aren't very large, our deliverable will be able to, by zone in the plant and using a worker's voice, be able to tell exactly how long a client's work order took to fix from beginning to end. By having this data available, companies and manafacturing sites can determine and define their financials accurately, allocate money and resources more effectively and, in the long run, save company resources.
